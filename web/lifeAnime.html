Suggestions

Life anime, or slice of Life, are animes based on characters living everyday life and dealing with the ups and downs. These can be relaxing, funny, or serious or a mix. The main storyline is usually episodic in nature with each episode having a distinct little storyline.A note of order is that many are related to school life, usually high school college, but also some lead into work life.

Wotakoi: Love is Hard for Otaku

A cute fun story about adult office workers and their struggles with their nerdy, or otaku loves. They live their everyday lives trying to get through the day while enjoying what they enjoy, and finding love while doing so as well. This is a anime that focuses on the relationships between otakus, and how they deal with it and work, and everyday life
